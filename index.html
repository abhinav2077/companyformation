We're a Companies House approved agent

Since we began we have pioneered registering businesses online and become an approved E-filing of Companies House,
the body responsible for UK limited company formation.

Quick and easy
company formation

Our experienced team offers a straight-forward and efficient way to register a
limited company with Companies House. If you are looking to scale up your
business as a limited company, we at The Company Formation UK can make
the process very convenient and cost-effective. The whole process is online,
no manual signatures are required.

Details needed

We make the task very easy for you with only the mandatory details being
asked, such as the name and address of the Company and personal details of
the directors(s) and the shareholder (s). Once the Company formation
application is approved Companies House and the same is
incorporated, we will send you the necessary documents such as
company's incorporation certificate, share certificate and memorandum
& articles of association by email.
